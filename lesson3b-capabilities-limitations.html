Capabilities & Limitations

Lesson 3B: Getting to know your new "AI Colleague"

The Versatile Colleague (Strengths)

✅ Language Mastery

Skilled at capturing specific voices, condensing reports into summaries, and translating complex topics across fields (microbiology to marketing).

✅ Contextual Memory

Maintains the "thread" of a conversation, remembering earlier details and project deadlines discussed within the same session.

The "Generalist" Edge: Unlike traditional software, the same model can write poetry, analyze business trends, and help with coding without needing separate training for each task.

The Boundaries (Limitations)

⚠️ The Fact Gap

AI doesn't "verify" facts during training. It follows statistical patterns, which can lead to Hallucinations—confidently stating plausible but incorrect info.

⚠️ Reasoning Hurdles

Historically struggles with multi-step math or complex logic, though "Extended Thinking" models are currently bridging this gap.

Knowledge Cutoff The date when training ended. The AI is "in a retreat" and doesn't know what happened after this date without web tools.
Non-Deterministic Ask the same question twice, get different answers. AI makes probabilistic decisions, not fixed ones.
Temperature A setting used to control the randomness or "creativity" of the AI's responses.
