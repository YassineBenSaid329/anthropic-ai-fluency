# AI Fluency Course - Project Plan

## Project Overview

**Project Title:** AI-Military Ethics Landscape Research  
**Main Goal:** Build knowledge on AI governance through the Anthropic-Pentagon case  
**Deliverable:** Well-organized HTML page with structured research breakdown  
**Time Estimate:** ~1 hour of work

---

## Project Vision

### What Success Looks Like
- Become knowledgeable about AI governance, military AI use, and power dynamics between companies and governments
- Create a comprehensive "map" of the landscape covering:
  - Technical aspects (how AI is used in weapons)
  - Ethical dimensions (boundaries and debates)
  - Political dynamics (government vs private companies)
  - Economic factors (what gives companies leverage)

### Why This Project Matters
- Understanding AI ethics and governance - crucial for working with AI
- Learning how to research complex, multi-faceted topics
- Building skills in synthesizing information from multiple perspectives

---

## Major Tasks Breakdown

### 1. Research & Information Gathering
**What:** Find reliable sources about the Anthropic-Pentagon situation, military AI use, ethics frameworks, and stakeholder positions

**Delegation:**
- **AI handles:** Search and find sources, locate articles/reports/statements
- **Human handles:** Decide depth needed, judge relevance, determine when enough sources collected
- **Collaborate:** Iterative process - AI finds → Human guides direction → AI refines search

---

### 2. Deep Dive & Analysis
**What:** Read and understand sources, analyze perspectives, identify patterns, synthesize insights

**Delegation:**
- **AI handles:** Summarize content, identify patterns across sources, spot contradictions
- **Human handles:** Ask critical questions, detect bias, probe deeper, focus attention on what matters most
- **Collaborate:** Dialogue-driven - AI reveals patterns → Human questions → AI digs deeper based on curiosity

---

### 3. Organizing & Structuring
**What:** Decide how to break down information, create logical flow, structure sections and navigation

**Delegation:**
- **AI handles:** Suggest organizational frameworks and section structures, propose multiple options
- **Human handles:** Choose preferred structure, decide final sections and order
- **Collaborate:** AI proposes options → Human selects what resonates → AI refines

---

### 4. Content Creation
**What:** Write actual text for each section, ensure clarity and balance, cite sources

**Delegation:**
- **AI handles:** Draft balanced text, cite sources appropriately, make requested edits
- **Human handles:** Review for accuracy and balance, request changes, approve final content
- **Collaborate:** AI drafts → Human reviews → AI revises based on feedback

---

### 5. HTML Development
**What:** Design page layout, code HTML/CSS, create navigation, ensure readability

**Delegation:**
- **AI handles:** Build simple and functional HTML/CSS with navigation structure
- **Human handles:** Review and approve design, test functionality
- **Collaborate:** AI creates → Human approves or requests adjustments

---

## Key Insights from Planning

### About Delegation
- AI works best when human brings expertise and context
- Need both **Problem Awareness** (clear goals) and **Platform Awareness** (understanding what AI can do)
- Most valuable work happens in **augmentation** mode (collaborating together), not just automation

### Personal Learning
From n8n experience: Jumping into complex projects without platform knowledge leads to struggle. Better approach:
1. First use AI to learn the platform/tool
2. Then collaborate on building projects with realistic expectations
