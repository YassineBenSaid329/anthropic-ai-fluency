The 4D Framework

Lesson 2: Core Competencies for AI Collaboration

🎙️ Rick Dacon

Future-Proofing Your Skills

No matter how you interact with AI—through automation, augmentation, or agency—there are four essential competencies that guarantee effective, efficient, ethical, and safe collaboration. These are not tied to specific tools or fleeting prompt techniques; they are fundamental, adaptable skills designed to grow alongside rapidly evolving technology.

🗺️

1. Delegation

Focuses on the big picture and strategic division of work. It's not just about offloading tasks; it's about having a clear vision of your goals.

  • Understand the problem you are solving.
  • Recognize what AI can and cannot do well.
  • Decide what tasks to hand off (e.g., data review) vs. what to keep for yourself (e.g., critical analysis & final conclusions).
🗣️

2. Description

Focuses on clear, detailed, and context-rich communication. It goes far beyond simply "writing prompts."

  • Establish your ultimate goals and desired output format.
  • Provide necessary background context and information.
  • Specify the exact tone, style, and approach you want the AI to take (e.g., "Don't give me the answer, act as a tutor and guide me").
🧐

3. Discernment

Focuses on thoughtfully evaluating what the AI produces. This requires your personal domain expertise and critical insight.

  • Verify if the facts are accurate and the reasoning is logical.
  • Check if the recommendations align with your values.
  • Determine if the output actually helps you move forward, needs refinement, or should be discarded entirely.
🛡️

4. Diligence

Focuses on responsible, safe, and ethical AI interactions. It means taking full ownership of your AI-assisted work.

  • Control for potential biases and ensure fairness.
  • Protect sensitive data and privacy.
  • Maintain transparency about the involvement of AI in your final product.
  • Be fully accountable for the final results.

🔄 The Collaboration Loop

Most of our interactions with AI do not happen in a single step. They rely on continuous, small loops between two of the core competencies:

Description ➔ AI Output ➔ Discernment ➔ Refine Request ➔ 🔁

Describing what we need ➔ Evaluating what we get ➔ Refining our request.
