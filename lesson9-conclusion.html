The Complete AI Fluency Framework

🤝

Delegation

Deciding what work AI should do versus what humans should handle alone.

  • Creation: Thoughtfully choosing AI systems.
  • Transparency: Being open about AI's role.
  • Deployment: Taking ownership of outputs.
💬

Description

Clearly communicating your needs, process, and desired interaction style to the AI.

  • Product: Defining WHAT you want.
  • Process: Guiding HOW the AI works.
  • Performance: Setting the BEHAVIORAL style.
🔍

Discernment

Critically evaluating AI outputs, reasoning, and interactive behaviors.

  • Product: Judging the final output.
  • Process: Assessing the AI's reasoning.
  • Performance: Evaluating the interaction itself.
🛡️

Diligence

Ensuring your AI interactions are responsible, ethical, and safe.

  • Creation: Thoughtfully choosing AI systems.
  • Transparency: Being open about AI's role.
  • Deployment: Taking ownership of outputs.

Essential Insights to Carry Forward

🤝 On Delegation

Your expertise is the foundation. The best outcomes emerge from iterative collaboration where you and the AI build on each other's strengths.

💬 On Description

Clear communication is the bridge. It's a thoughtful conversation with context and examples, not just elaborate prompts.

🔍 On Discernment

This is a non-negotiable responsibility. Your critical eye protects against AI limitations and elevates the quality of the final product.

🛡️ On Diligence

You are always accountable. Being transparent about AI's role in your work is essential for building trust and integrity.

Your Journey Continues

Fluency develops through practice. As you move forward, remember to be:

  • Effective
  • Efficient
  • Ethical
  • Safe
