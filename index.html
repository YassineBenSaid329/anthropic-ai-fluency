About the Course

AI Fluency: Framework & Foundations is a professional curriculum developed by Anthropic in partnership with academic experts Prof. Joseph Feller (University College Cork) and Prof. Rick Dakan (Ringling College).

The course establishes a multidisciplinary model for human-AI collaboration, helping users interact with systems like Claude in ways that are effective, efficient, ethical, and safe.
