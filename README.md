## 💡 Core Philosophy
This curriculum shifts the focus away from AI as purely a *technology* (or chasing the latest prompt-engineering fad) and focuses on **Human-AI Interaction**. The goal is to stop thinking *about* AI and start learning how to **think *with* AI** as a trusted partner in creative problem-solving.

### The 4 D's of AI Interaction
The notes cover the core competencies required to engage with AI effectively, efficiently, ethically, and safely:
1. 🤝 **Delegation:** When should humans do the work, and when should AI?
2. 💬 **Description:** How do we communicate clearly with AI systems?
3. 🔍 **Discernment:** How do we evaluate what AI gives us?
4. 🛡️ **Diligence:** How do we ensure our interaction is responsible?
