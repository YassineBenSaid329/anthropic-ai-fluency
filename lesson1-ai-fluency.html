Core Philosophy

This course is not just about AI as a technology, but about human-AI interaction. Instead of viewing AI as a "next-level spellchecker" or chasing fleeting prompt engineering fads, the goal is to treat AI as a trusted partner for creative and innovative problem-solving.

What is AI Fluency?
The ability to engage with AI systems in ways that are effective, efficient, ethical, and safe. The ultimate goal is to change how we think about AI so we can learn to think with AI.

The 4 D's of AI Interaction

To bridge the gap between what's possible and what feels comfortable, the curriculum introduces four core competencies rather than tactical, quickly outdated technical tricks:

🤝

1. Delegation

When should humans do the work, and when should AI? Understanding the boundaries of appropriate tasks for AI systems vs. human expertise.

💬

2. Description

How do we communicate clearly with AI systems? The skill of accurately expressing intent, context, and goals to an AI partner.

🔍

3. Discernment

How do we evaluate what AI gives us? Developing the critical thinking required to judge the accuracy, relevance, and quality of AI outputs.

🛡️

4. Diligence

How do we ensure our interaction is responsible? Maintaining transparency, accountability, and ethical considerations in all AI collaborations.

Expected Course Outcomes

  • A Guiding Framework: Navigate AI beyond tactical prompt engineering tips.
  • Decisive Confidence: Know exactly when and how to engage AI effectively.
  • Practical Collaboration Skills: Achieve fluid, thoughtful human-AI partnerships.
  • Accountability: Gain the ability to evaluate and take full responsibility for the outcomes of your AI collaborations.
